BSTOPPT Newborn Sleep Sack

Give your baby the peaceful sleep they deserve with this safe and cozy sleep sack. Perfect for 3-6 month olds, it's designed to keep your little one comfortable and secure throughout the night.

Breathable and Gentle

Constructed from 100% rayon cotton, this sleep sack is soft, breathable, and ideal for sensitive skin. Its airy fabric ensures all-night comfort without irritation, making it a great choice for mild weather.

3-Way Adjustable Swaddle

This swaddling blanket features a 3-way adjustable system that lets you customize the fit for your baby's needs. Whether you're swaddling them fully, allowing one arm out for self-soothing, or transitioning to a sleep bag, the design adapts seamlessly.

The secure arm and neck closures offer a snug fit, while the sleeveless style helps regulate body temperature and reduces the risk of overheating. It's the perfect solution for parents looking to keep their baby safe and comfortable during sleep.

Easy Diaper Changes with Reverse Zipper

The reverse zipper design makes diaper changes a breeze without waking your baby. This feature adds convenience to your nighttime routine and ensures uninterrupted sleep for your little one.

Seasonal Adjustments

With a TOG rating of 0.5, this sleep sack is ideal for warm seasons. However, if your baby needs extra warmth, it can be upgraded to TOG 1.0 for greater protection. This versatility makes it a smart investment for parents.

Safe and Reliable

As a practical alternative to loose blankets in the crib, this sleep sack reduces the risk of SIDS. It's designed with your baby's safety in mind and is safe to use with a secure, snug fit throughout the night.
